Purpose

The purpose of this Rafting Site Plan is to provide a pleasant browsing experience to the visitor for research of available short and long rafting excursions for individuals or groups.

Who are the target customers?

The target customers include families who are looking for vacaton activities on water, they also include tourists and also sportsmen who engage in water sports/ rafting as a sport.

What do they want?

These People want to have access to prior knowledge of what services are provided at tr water rafting site.

How will my audience usually access my site?

For now, my audience will only have access to my site via a desktop computer or a laptop. In the mean tine, they'll also be able to access the site with their mobile devices.
